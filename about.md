  <h1>About Me</h1>

  <p>
    I am Harish Kumar, a mathematics teacher based in Hong Kong with experience teaching middle school, high school, and IB mathematics. My work has included teaching across a range of year levels and supporting students with different learning needs, academic goals, and levels of confidence in mathematics.
  </p>

  <p>
    I believe that strong mathematics teaching combines clarity, challenge, and care. In my classroom, I aim to help students build conceptual understanding, communicate their reasoning clearly, and approach problems with confidence and persistence. I value lessons that are well-structured, intellectually engaging, and responsive to student needs.
  </p>

  <p>
    My teaching experience includes working with diverse learners in international school settings and teaching curricula that emphasize both deep understanding and application. I am particularly interested in helping students move beyond memorizing procedures toward thinking mathematically, making connections, and becoming more independent learners.
  </p>

  <p>
    Across all classes, I aim to create a classroom culture that is calm, focused, respectful, and supportive. I believe students do their best learning when expectations are high, routines are clear, and they feel safe to ask questions, make mistakes, and improve.
  </p>

  <p>
    This portfolio shares my teaching philosophy, professional experience, and selected resources that reflect my approach to teaching and learning.
  </p>
</div>
